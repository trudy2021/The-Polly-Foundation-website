About Us

Community based initiative

We are a community based initiative located in Siaya county, Kenya. We are determined to work hand in hand with the community to solve some of the numerous problems that we face in our societies today.
We advocate for ecosystem recovery through tree planting activities and environmental conservation campaigns.

Supporting small scale farmers.

At the Polly Foundation, we engage, support small scale farmers and youths to build one another. We respect all, embrace diversity, inclusion for everyone pushing for action in the community. We also share opportunities, develop common position papers, nature making a better world for all.

Projects

Bio Farm

Bio Farm Pesticide

For us to achieve the ecosystem restoration, there is need to use more ecofriendly farm inputs. Biofarm project aims at formulation of an organic pesticide(Biopesticide) that will help us reduce the amount of toxic chemicals in the environment.

Community health

Mental Health

For effective productivity of a community and its members, There is necessity for mental fitness within the community members. The mental health project aims at sensitizing and educating the community on the best practices for eradication of mental illness in our communities.

Afforestation

Treeplanting

Since we are working towards achieving a healthier environment, planting more trees and increasing forest cover is a necessity to a natural and healthy environment. The main goal of this project is restoration and conservation of the natural environment.

Agri-tech

Automatic digital sprayer

The project aims at elevating green house and screen house farming methods in technological aspect where the green houses and screen houses will be fitted with a technology system that can automatically detect the presence of pest by motion sensing capabilities.

Organic Products

Moringa soap
